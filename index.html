VLSI Design & Engineering

Very Large Scale Integration (VLSI) is at the heart of modern electronics, enabling the creation of powerful, energy-efficient chips for computing, networking, and AI. Our VLSI services include:

  • ASIC/FPGA Design: End-to-end design, verification, and prototyping of custom integrated circuits.
  • RTL Design & Synthesis: Register-transfer level design using industry-standard HDLs (Verilog, VHDL).
  • Physical Design & DFT: Layout, timing analysis, and design-for-testability for robust silicon implementation.
  • IP Core Development: Custom and reusable intellectual property blocks for SoC integration.
  • Prototyping & Validation: Rapid prototyping and hardware validation for faster time-to-market.

Our VLSI team collaborates with semiconductor foundries and EDA tool vendors to deliver world-class solutions for clients in AI, IoT, automotive, and consumer electronics.
